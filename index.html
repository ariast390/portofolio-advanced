Software Engineer Internship

YABB-GOTO

Feb - Agu 2022

GOTO company, a collaboration between Gojek and Tokopedia, offers a specialized learning program and bootcamp specifically designed for aspiring frontend engineers. Known as the Frontend Engineer Student Training, this comprehensive program aims to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the field.

The program spans a total duration of 5 months, providing participants with an immersive learning experience. The first three months are dedicated to technical skills training, where students delve deep into various aspects of frontend engineering. They learn and master essential tools, technologies, and frameworks utilized in frontend development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS. Through hands-on projects and practical exercises, students gain proficiency in building interactive and responsive user interfaces.

In addition to technical skills, the program also emphasizes the importance of soft skills and career readiness. Participants receive guidance and training to enhance their communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and time management abilities. They also acquire valuable insights into the industry, including resume writing, interview preparation, and professional etiquette. This holistic approach ensures that students are well-rounded and equipped to thrive in their future frontend engineering careers.

During the final two months of the program, students engage in a capstone project or internship. This component provides them with real-world experience, allowing them to apply their acquired knowledge and skills to practical scenarios. They work on challenging projects under the guidance of experienced mentors, gaining hands-on experience and further honing their abilities as frontend engineers.

Overall, the Frontend Engineer Student Training program by GOTO offers a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum, combining technical skills training, soft skills development, and practical experience. By the end of the 5-month program, participants are equipped with the necessary tools and competencies to excel as frontend engineers in the dynamic and evolving tech industry.
